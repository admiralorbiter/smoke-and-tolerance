Alchemical stoichiometry: Standard black powder reactions yield roughly 45% gas product (expanding carbonic fumes and nitrogen spirit) and 55% solid residue (alkali carbonated salts, sulfate ash, and sulfurous crust). Saltpeter purity scales the gas yield; low purity produces heavy damp residue and slow, lethargic pressure.
